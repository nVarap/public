Slate

Slate was one of the most project that I have ever done. It required months of research, along with hours of painstaking time throughout the process of the project. There were 3 main steps to Slate.


First there were a lot of research that needed to be completed in order to ensure that this could be completed. First of all, I needed to look into Tensorflow and figure out how their CNN (Conventional Neural Network) libraries worked. This involved researching how CNNs worked in general.
